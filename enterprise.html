Banty Enterprise

Brand Focused Rooms

Maintain your brand’s exposure during your team’s virtual meetings. Banty Enterprise’s custom video conferences allow you to customize the room’s color to coordinate with your brand and you can even place your business’ logo anywhere you like. Banty offers a customized video chat experience that compliments your brand while bolstering brand recognition. 

Own Your Room

Name your room and it’s yours forever. With Banty, you don’t have to worry about sending a new link out every time you need to video chat with your colleagues. Simply name your video chat room whatever you like, and you and your colleagues can easily access it whenever you need it. 

Productive Online Meetings

Host anything from a small executive meeting to a 20 person online video conference without sacrificing the video and audio that makes your team look and sound great. Our servers can handle what you throw at them, guaranteeing your business can host the online video calls you need with no virtual hangups. Just send the video chat participants the link they need, and one-click and they’re in! 

Security and Privacy

Banty’s Enterprise video conferencing features top-of-the-line security and privacy. Our servers are secure and all communications are continuously encrypted “hop-by-hop”. We even offer the ability to host the video router on servers dedicated to your organization, because safer is better. Keep your business and employees safe from unwelcome eyes with Banty. 

Easier Than Facebook

Banty’s one-click video chat ensures even your least technologically-adept colleagues will have no issue accessing your business’ video chat rooms. Establish the secure and easy-to-use video conferencing your business needs. 

Get Help When You Need It

Banty’s easy to use and secure video chatting is a streamlined one-click process, but we know even the most well-designed technology can go wrong at inopportune times. Don’t worry though, our team is caring and compassionate. If you need help, we’re here and have your back!
